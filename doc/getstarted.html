CLICK-UP: Get Started



In order to further demonstrate the effectiveness and convenience of CLICK-UP, we offer a software upgrade walkthrough of firewall network element. We hope to demonstrate the agility of module integration by including a large number of new modules upgraded in the demo, and checking its downtime compared to traditional manners. We also plan to demonstrate the state persistency by including tcp session states in the demo, and observing the negative influence of state loss compared to our state reconstruction case.
